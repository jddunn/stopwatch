StopWatch

Automatically curated content from YouTube based on your emotions in real-time.

A video that makes you happy, surprised, or at the very least engaged? Then a similar one to it will be played next. If that video makes you sad or disgusted or angry, then it'll skip and a different one in your playlist will automatically play. Watch video content intelligently and lazily.

A webcam is required for the site to recognize your facial expressions.
If nothing happens when you try building a playlist, it means your specified keywords do not produce enough content (50 videos) for viewing. Try something else.
